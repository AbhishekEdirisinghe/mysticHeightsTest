Traditional Medicine & Wellness at Mystique Heights

Wide in scope and rich in diversity, Sri Lankan traditional medicine is a combination of indigenous medical practices, known as Paramparika Vedakama. Today, we refer to it as "Sri Lankan Traditional Medicine," and it has retained its unique identity over generations, predating the era of modern medicines.

Diagnostic Approach and Personalized Treatment

Traditional doctors use a patient's pulse to diagnose ailments, which is a fascinating experience in itself. They provide personalized treatment that takes into account various factors, such as the individual's current state, age, and country of residence. Traditional doctors use natural elements like leaves, bark, flowers, roots, and fruits to treat ailments.

Medicinal Plants and Natural Remedies

Sri Lanka is a country blessed with a wide variety of medicinal trees and plants. Medicines are prepared using herbs, leaves, tree bark, roots, and other natural substances. The diverse range of leaves, vegetables, and fruits in Sri Lanka is rich in nutritional value and plays a crucial role in ensuring the effectiveness of traditional treatment practices.

Holistic Practices for Health and Wellness

Patients are educated about different dietary patterns and simple meditation practices that lead to a balanced life and a healthy mind, in harmony with Mother Nature. Along with medication, these practices promote overall wellness. Our bodies are not meant to accumulate toxins caused by improper digestion, high levels of stress, and environmental pollution. Water and food can continuously introduce toxins into the body. If not effectively and regularly flushed out, toxin buildup can eventually manifest as various disorders.

Body Treatments and Massages

Along with the medicinal treatments and meditation practices, body massages are performed using different types of oils, depending on the body type. These massages are highly beneficial for general rejuvenation, skin and musculoskeletal conditioning, and addressing issues such as obesity, body aches, memory enhancement, fat reduction, skin tone improvement, and promoting restful sleep.
